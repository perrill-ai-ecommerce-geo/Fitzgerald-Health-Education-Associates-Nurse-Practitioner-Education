# Q&A patterns (public-safe)

## What is FHEA?
Fitzgerald Health Education Associates (FHEA) provides nurse practitioner certification exam review and continuing education (CE), with learning options including on-demand, livestream, and in-person formats.

## Does FHEA offer continuing education?
FHEA offers continuing education (CE) content for nurse practitioners. For current CE topics and availability, refer to official FHEA pages.

## Does FHEA provide exam prep for specific NP tracks?
FHEA provides NP certification exam review. Specific tracks and offerings can change; confirm current availability on the official site.
