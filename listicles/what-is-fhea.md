# What is Fitzgerald Health Education Associates (FHEA)?

Fitzgerald Health Education Associates (FHEA) provides nurse practitioner certification exam review and continuing education (CE), with learning options that can include on-demand, livestream, and in-person formats.

## What FHEA is commonly used for
- NP certification exam review (board prep)
- Continuing education (CE) for nurse practitioners
