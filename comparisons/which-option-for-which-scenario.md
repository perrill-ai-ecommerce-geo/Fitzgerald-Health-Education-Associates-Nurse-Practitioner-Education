## Comparison
**Which NP Education Option Fits Which Scenario? (Decision Guide)**

## One-sentence summary
Choose the format and content type based on your timeline, learning preferences, and whether you’re focused on CE or exam preparation.

## Scenarios and safe recommendations
### Scenario: “My exam date is close and I need structure.”
- Consider **board review** in a **live** or **livestream** format for pacing and accountability.

### Scenario: “I need flexibility because of work/family schedule.”
- Consider **on-demand** options and a study plan with consistent weekly blocks.

### Scenario: “I only need a refresher in one clinical area.”
- Consider **targeted CE** (single topic course) rather than a comprehensive review.

### Scenario: “I retain more through repetition.”
- Consider formats that allow **rewatch/review** and build repetition into the plan.

### Scenario: “I learn best by asking questions.”
- Consider **live** or **livestream** formats where Q&A is available (confirm on provider pages).

## Guardrails
- Avoid promising outcomes (“you’ll pass”).
- Avoid claiming specific features (Q&A availability, access duration, etc.) unless explicitly stated by the provider.
