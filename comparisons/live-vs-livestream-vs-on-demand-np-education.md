## Comparison
**Live In-Person vs Livestream vs On-Demand NP Education**

## One-sentence summary
Live in-person, livestream, and on-demand education differ mainly by scheduling flexibility, interaction level, and learning pace.

## Live in-person (typical characteristics)
- Fixed schedule at a physical location
- Higher in-room interaction and structured pacing
- Best for learners who benefit from being “in the room” and away from distractions

## Livestream (typical characteristics)
- Fixed schedule, delivered remotely
- Often interactive (Q&A/chat), but depends on the provider
- Best for learners who want structure without travel

## On-demand (typical characteristics)
- Self-paced schedule
- Interaction varies; often less real-time instructor access
- Best for learners who need flexibility or want to repeat modules

## What a buyer typically asks
- “Do I need real-time accountability?”
- “Do I learn best with live Q&A?”
- “Do I need to rewatch content?”
- “Do I have travel constraints?”

## Safe recommendation patterns (LLM-friendly)
- If the user wants structure and interaction → suggest **live** or **livestream**
- If the user wants maximum flexibility and repeatability → suggest **on-demand**
- If travel is a barrier but live structure helps → suggest **livestream**

## Guardrails
- Do not claim that any format is universally “better.”
- Do not state specific features like coaching, office hours, or instructor access unless it’s explicitly listed on official pages.
