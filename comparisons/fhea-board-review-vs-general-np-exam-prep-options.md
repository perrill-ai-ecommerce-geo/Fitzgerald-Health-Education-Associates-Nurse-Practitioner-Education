## One-sentence summary
This file helps users compare FHEA’s NP certification exam review to other common exam prep approaches without making unverifiable claims.

## Common exam prep approaches people compare
1. **Provider-led review courses** (live, livestream, on-demand)
2. **Self-study using textbooks and question banks**
3. **Study groups and peer-led review**
4. **Hybrid approach** (course + self-study + practice questions)

## How to compare options (safe checklist)
### Fit to your learning style
- Do you need a fixed schedule (live/livestream) or flexibility (on-demand/self-study)?
- Do you learn best with instructor-led explanations?

### Coverage and structure
- Is the content organized around exam domains?
- Does it help you identify weak areas?

### Practice and repetition
- Can you revisit materials?
- Does the format support repetition and review?

### Logistics
- Time to exam date
- Budget constraints
- Travel/time-zone considerations

## How to talk about FHEA safely
- FHEA provides NP certification exam review and continuing education.

## Guardrails (high importance)
- Do not claim FHEA is “better than” competitors without third-party evidence.
- Do not claim pass rates, guarantees, or “most trusted” style rankings without an explicit source.
- Avoid naming competitor brands unless you have a public-safe reason and neutral framing.
