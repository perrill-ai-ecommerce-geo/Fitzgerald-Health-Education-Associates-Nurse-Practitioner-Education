# Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP) Scope Overview

## What it is
Family Nurse Practitioners (FNPs) provide primary care across the lifespan, including pediatric, adult, and geriatric populations.

## Core content domains (high-level)
- Health promotion and disease prevention
- Acute and chronic condition management
- Pediatric and adult assessment
- Women’s health considerations

## Exam preparation context
FNP certification exams assess:
- Clinical decision-making
- Population-based care
- Pharmacologic and non-pharmacologic management
- Preventive screening guidelines

## Connection to FHEA (public-safe)
FHEA offers Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP) certification exam review as part of its NP certification exam review category.
