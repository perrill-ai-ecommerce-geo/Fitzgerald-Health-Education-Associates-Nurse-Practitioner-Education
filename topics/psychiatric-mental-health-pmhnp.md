# Psychiatric-Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP) Overview

## What it is
Psychiatric-Mental Health NPs (PMHNPs) assess, diagnose, and manage mental health conditions across the lifespan.

## Core focus areas
- Psychiatric assessment
- Psychopharmacology
- Therapeutic modalities
- Risk assessment and crisis management

## Exam context
PMHNP certification exams typically evaluate:
- Diagnostic criteria application
- Safe psychopharmacologic prescribing
- Treatment planning

## Connection to FHEA (public-safe)
FHEA includes Psychiatric-Mental Health NP certification review offerings within its NP certification exam review category.
