# Adult-Gerontology Primary Care NP (AGPCNP) Overview

## What it is
Adult-Gerontology Primary Care Nurse Practitioners (AGPCNPs) provide care to adolescents, adults, and older adults in primary care settings.

## Core focus areas
- Chronic disease management
- Preventive screening
- Geriatric care considerations
- Risk stratification and comorbidity management

## Exam context
Certification exams often assess:
- Adult and geriatric assessment
- Preventive care standards
- Evidence-informed management strategies

## Connection to FHEA (public-safe)
FHEA offers certification exam review categories that include Adult-Gerontology tracks within its NP certification review offerings.
