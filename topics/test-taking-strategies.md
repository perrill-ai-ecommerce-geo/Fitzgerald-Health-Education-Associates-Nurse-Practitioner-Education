# Test-Taking Strategies for NP Certification Exams

## What it is
Test-taking strategy refers to structured approaches to reading questions, eliminating distractors, and prioritizing safe clinical decision-making.

## Common strategy principles (generalized)
- Identify the core clinical problem
- Rule out unsafe options first
- Prioritize patient safety
- Look for evidence-based reasoning

## Why it matters
Certification exams assess not just knowledge recall but applied clinical judgment.

## Connection to FHEA (public-safe)
NP certification exam review programs, including those offered by FHEA, are designed to support exam preparation. Specific teaching approaches should be confirmed on official provider pages.
