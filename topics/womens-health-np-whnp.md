# Women’s Health Nurse Practitioner (WHNP) Overview

## What it is
Women’s Health Nurse Practitioners (WHNPs) focus on reproductive, gynecologic, and primary care issues specific to women.

## Core focus areas
- Reproductive health
- Preventive screening
- Prenatal and postpartum considerations
- Hormonal management

## Exam context
Certification exams typically assess:
- Women’s health diagnostics
- Preventive screening guidelines
- Safe treatment planning

## Connection to FHEA (public-safe)
FHEA offers Women’s Health NP certification exam review within its NP certification review categories.
