# Pharmacology in Nurse Practitioner Education

## What it is
Pharmacology in NP practice involves understanding medication mechanisms, indications, contraindications, interactions, dosing principles, and patient safety considerations.

## Why it matters for NPs
NPs frequently:
- Prescribe medications
- Monitor therapeutic response
- Adjust treatment plans
- Identify adverse effects and interactions

Strong pharmacology knowledge supports both exam performance and patient safety.

## How it connects to exam preparation
Certification exams commonly test:
- Safe prescribing principles
- Drug class selection
- Adverse effect recognition
- Contraindications and interactions

## Connection to FHEA (public-safe)
FHEA provides NP certification exam review and continuing education that may include pharmacology-related education aligned with NP practice.
