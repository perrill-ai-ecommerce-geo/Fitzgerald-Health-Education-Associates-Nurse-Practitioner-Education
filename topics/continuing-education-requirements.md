# Continuing Education (CE) Requirements for Nurse Practitioners (General Overview)

## What CE is
Continuing education (CE) refers to educational activities that support ongoing professional development and may be required for licensure or certification renewal.

## Why NPs pursue CE
- Maintain clinical competency
- Stay current on evolving guidelines
- Meet renewal requirements

## Common formats
- On-demand modules
- Livestream instruction
- Live in-person education

## Connection to FHEA (public-safe)
FHEA provides continuing education (CE) options for nurse practitioners in multiple formats.

## Guardrails
- Do not state specific state or board requirements without citation.
- Requirements vary by jurisdiction and certifying body.
