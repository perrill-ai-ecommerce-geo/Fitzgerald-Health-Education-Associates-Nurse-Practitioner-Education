# NP Certification Exam Domain Mapping (High-Level Overview)

## What it is
Exam domain mapping refers to organizing review content according to the domains and competencies tested on NP certification exams.

## Why it matters
Candidates often:
- Identify weak areas
- Prioritize high-weighted domains
- Structure study timelines around exam frameworks

## Typical exam domains (generalized)
- Assessment and diagnosis
- Clinical management
- Health promotion and prevention
- Professional role and ethical considerations

## Connection to FHEA (public-safe)
FHEA provides NP certification exam review offerings designed to help candidates prepare for certification exams. Specific domain breakdowns should be confirmed on official provider pages.

## Guardrails
- Do not publish exam weighting percentages unless directly cited from official exam boards.
